What Exactly is an AI Desk Bot?

An **AI Desk Bot** is an advanced software application powered by artificial intelligence, designed to act as a virtual assistant within a workspace. Unlike traditional chatbots, these bots leverage technologies like **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **Machine Learning (ML)** to understand complex queries, perform tasks, and interact in a highly intelligent and autonomous manner.

Their primary goal is to **streamline operations**, **reduce manual workload**, and **enhance productivity** by handling routine tasks, providing instant information, and automating communication, thereby freeing up human employees for more strategic work.
